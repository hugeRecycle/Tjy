Administrative Assistant
Wei Yang 杨微

Wei Yang was born in Zhuji, Zhejiang Province. She joined Jingyi Tian’s group in 2024 as administrative assistant at Westlake University. Before that, she earned her bachelor’s degree in Tianjin Foreign Studies University，and then she received her master’s degree from Zhejiang University of Technology. She has many years of working experience in administrative management and she is now responsible for the all-round administrative duties in Jingyi Tian‘s group. In her spare time, Wei Yang enjoys traveling and listing to the music.
